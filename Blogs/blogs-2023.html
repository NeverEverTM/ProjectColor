        In Firelight you play as a small moth called Toffee, waking up in a mysterious forgotten cave, 
        traverse kingdoms and towns through the underground cavern, meet colorful friends and foes, and may the light guide your way.

        A bullet hell action adventure metroidvania, a more frentic style of combat mixed with inmersive exploration.

        The world will consist of three main mega-zones, made up of other various sub-areas, secrets everywhere you go
        and of course, enemies to defeat in combat, be it though foes or bosses.

        In terms of progression, there would be more-or-less a linear path of beating the story, 
        however, if im able to, I would like to let the player choose their way, via designing difficulty around player level and skill. 
        Perhaps even certain items helping you on a different area if done first...
        
        Apart from the obvious ways of player progression in a metroidvania, you will also have a medal skill tree to work with.
        Level up to become stronger, and find medals with all sorts of effects, upragde them, and branch them out.

        A few inspirations for this game has been:
        * Hollow Knight for its vast cavern system thatinvited you to explore an unknown world full of adventure.
        * Bug Fables, both in story and character writting, a more lightheartead, yet profound dialogue between characters and 
            memorable interactions.
        * Crosscode for its amazing combat system, a personalized journey of new powers and abilities that unlock as you go through.

        Less to say that I recommend anyone to try out those amazing indie gems, specially the last two, those go very unnoticed :)

        In A Light Away, you play as Will, who is visiting his grandmother resting place at the top of Mount Reyn, 
        just to see her one last time. Along the way you will meet Wisp, a stubborn yet joyful light gal
        who wants to see the aurora event at the top, that only happens once a century.
        A journey not quite easy mind you, but one they are willing to take.

        Climb the mountain in this simple percision platformer, face the dangers that are thrown at you, 
        and meet nice (and not so nice) people in your way.
        A story-driven campaign with various levels composed of hundreds of rooms full of challenges and secrets.

        And to top it off, a level editor! Yeah, I did not think about how big those two words are...
        Currently, A Light Away is on pause, as I develop Firelight, and get myself into the nitty-gritty of game dev.
        A lot of systems are in place, some others not so much, but it has been burnt me so much I needed a break from it.

        Definitely not anytime soon, but you will surely hear from time to time how I hammer things into place.
        If you want more of those, be sure to be on the discord for when I shadow-drop info on it!
