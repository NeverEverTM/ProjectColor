Aphid Festival Resort Update

The game has been updated to Godot 4.3, so there may be some unexpected consequences, but so far it looks like it works fine.

I have pushed major works on the game's infrastrcture, under the hood improvements for the changes to come, and while the implementations are still a bit green and undergoing testing, I'm glad to say most of it works fine enough!

The core of aphid's behaviour has been heavily modified to allow for a more diverse and unique personality for the lil guy, this first batch of updates includes Traits!

Traits affect their behaviour in all sorts of fun and different ways...

  • Maybe your aphid is a picky eater and won't eat food that is not their favorite...
  • Or they tend to be lazier and sleep far more than usual
  • Or they smarter than the others and can learn skills at a faster pace!

Beyond this, some other functions are planned to be implemented as the month ends and hopefully seen late January, this includes:

  • Skills that can be learned and used in tasks(already in-game but are currently unusued)
  • Relationships (Aphids would be able to interact between each other)
  • Likes and Dislikes (Aphids already have favorite flavours, but more favorites are planned to be added and further developed)

What else? A lot of polish and general QoL has been done.

Two issues that went unnoticed have been solved, one that made the continue button boot you into an empty map (fortunately, this seems to be completely harmless), and heavy peformance issues caused by torch lighting, which hopefully is solved or atleast softened.

Most of my sparetime was spent on retouching some of the menu layouts so they look prettier and become more functional overall!

Sound effects have been added to multiple things so the game should be a lot less silent than before, and along that, new settings to control audio more in general.

A lot of work is still underway but I hope to have showcase videos for january!
