With todays update, I bring a bit of QoL changes that were really needed back in 2.0. Hopefully by the next two months I can properly work in the big guns for AFR.

Until then, here's a brief resume of what's new for v0.2.1!

Generations Album and Aphid Utils

A functionality that was due for a while since the introduction of taking photos. The photo album!

The generations menu now has an album where you can see all photos taken, allowing you to even see individual photo albums for each aphid.

Balance Tweaks, Sprite Touchups and Performance Improvements

With a bit of grease and sweat, I got to the game to run more smoothly than last version, I am still finding bottlenecks in some areas but this
should hopefully soften problems of optimization and thus allow you to have as many aphids as you want.

Changes on aphid traits, stats and such have been made. The most noticeable will be a more subtle inheritance of colors unlike last update.
Hopefully aphid breeding becomes more refined as the updates come along, but I am happy with how things shaped up.

I also went ahead and added a brand new skin.

Between a few more things, some touchups to overall UX were on the works. This includes rebalancing of food and recipes.
None of the changes are permanent and require a revision on later updates but these changes should make them less busted than before
