Wake up as Toffee within the vast caverns halls of Lumi.
        Find the voice that echoes through your head and discover who you truly are.
            
        Delve deeper into its lands full of secrets and traps, fiends and thieves. 
        Featuring a world with three major areas that branch out onto multitude of different corners and towns.
                    
        Help its citizens along your way and meet all the diverse personalities of its bug kind, 
        from striking butterflies to wise flies, warrior wasps and greedy grasshopers, 
        ravaging roaches to bureaucratic beetles, and everything in between. 
            
        Master your skills in a fast-paced projectile-based combat, learn new abilites and arts as you grow,
        and branch out your possibilities. 
            
        A metroidvania bullet hell about the light that guides your way.
