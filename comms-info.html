Questions & Answers

How do I contact you?

If you go up, you will see the Contacts tab, from there you can reach me through direct messages on Discord, Bluesky and/or Devianart.

How much does the art cost change if I need XYZ thing?

This depends widly on a few different factors, specifics can be asked with no problem. Something like a sticker pack won't cost several hundred dollars but the complexity of the requirements may up the price

Can I hire you for XYZ project that I'm working for?

I'm up for hire if pay and conditions are fair and square. I don't do free jobs, nor I want to participate in your fan project if is not in my field of interests. If you want to do a collab of some sorts, first ask me directly if I'm free and interested in joining.

Do you do art trades/requests?

If I'm free, I am always up for a trade, requests not so much. Just don't expect me to say yes to all trades.

Can I draw your characters?

Go ahead! Just let me know so I can see it! I love people doing my goobers.

How do I pay you?/Can I pay you in Virtual Currencies/Steam Games/Nitro

For now I can only accept payments through Paypal, I will think to set up another ways for people unable to do so through this way. I do not accept alternative ways of payment, SPECIALLY for virtual currencies. No devianart points, no robux, no discord nitro. I cannot properly control these and will only put you and me at risk.

Can I share the pieces I paid for?

Yes! You have full control of your piece, wheter or not you want to credit me is up to you, however there is a few exceptions. You cannot claim you have made the piece, you cannot use it for AI training/NFT minting and you cannot use it for commercial use*. If my art is used as a front for controversial topics such as hate crimes, you will be blacklisted. I do not condone any kind of violent or abusive behaviour.

(*Depending on the case, an agreement needs to be reached beforehand)

Do you draw NSFW/suggestive art?

I do not do either NSFW or highly suggestive pieces, what counts as suggestive should be as simple as thinking straight for a moment, but if you are still not sure, you can ask me directly. If you are a minor, I will automatically deny you any kind of request of this type, plus be more strict about it.

Your art is too expensive, can't you do me a free one?

No, if you want a piece, save up money, don't come and ask for free art. I am already working in the lowest price ranges so I would appreciate it if you gave me a chance regardless.
